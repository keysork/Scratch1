General Info

  • Field hockey is a fast-paced team sport played on grass or turf
  • Two teams use curved sticks to hit a small, hard ball into the opposing team's goal
  • Each team has 11 players, including a goalkeeper
  • The game emphasizes speed, strategy, and stick skills

Where

  • It’s widely played around the world, especially popular in countries like:
    1. India
    2. Netherlands
    3. Australia
    4. United Kingdom

Skills

  • Dribbling – Moving the ball smoothly while maintaining control
  • Passing – Delivering the ball accurately to teammates
  • Receiving – Controlling and stopping the ball when passed
  • Shooting – Aiming and striking the ball to score
  • Tackling – Defending by legally taking the ball from opponents
  • Positioning – Being in the right place to support play
